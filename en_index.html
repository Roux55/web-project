THE ALGORITHMIC: WHAT IS IT?


Foreword

Our project will focus on the algorithmic currently studied in the modules M112Introduction to algorithmic and programming and M113Data structures and fundamental algorithms, each individually or in groups of two, will take care of their part and sub-parts. The first axis will be used to define and therefore, to understand what this that is the algorithmic, because of its history but also to know the use of the algorithmic within various fields. Subsequently, we will study the evolution and ubiquity of algorithmics in the world of economics and finance, but also among the population.

Thus for these facts and therefore by its importance, algorithmics is taught in higher education. An algorithm is comparable to a recipe: you have to do a precise number of actions in order to achieve a result. Therefore, the same approach is applied in other fields. We will see how the algorithmic has become essential to progress, how it is used and what its limits are.
