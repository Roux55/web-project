A. Assistance with calculations and forecasts


Algorithms find their place in many scientific fields since they can allow a series of calculations to be carried out infinitely faster than a human being. Thus, we find it in fields such as Mathematics but also in Physics or Chemistry. Thus, algorithmics allows man to interact with the machine through various languages, many specialized computer languages have been able to emerge.

B. «Machine learning» and artificial intelligence

Lately, algorithmics is an essential part of what makes up the neural network of what is called artificial intelligence or for short, AI.


Indeed, AI is a major element of the 21st century since it can be found in practically all connected objects. The so-called automatic learning algorithm will seek, through a series of complex computerised calculations and reasoning, to respond and adapt to your needs.


Thanks to this, AI is designed to evolve over time thanks to the data provided (machine learning based on the deep learning). This can be used to reconstruct, for example, an image where parts of it are missing or unrecognisable. (for more information, visit this article)


Nevertheless, the algorithm of artificial intelligence reaches its limits very far beyond the solving of mathematical problems since it does not allow to make ethical decisions contrary to us, human beings.

C. Considerable overstretching of human capacities

Thanks to the use of advanced technologies, combined with powerful algorithms, problem solving and anticipation have never been as precise and concise as they are today. Indeed, simple algorithms such as those used in Mathematics (e.g. simplex algorithm, algorithm for the calculation of a Gröbner base, graph theory, etc....) but also in Physics and in Chemistry (ex: algorithms of comparisons of molecular analyses, motion analysis and prediction, etc....) are resolved in a fraction of a second thanks to these.

Artificial intelligence takes its place in many cases such as mass data analysis: the Data Science which we will talk about soon.
